## What is a Item

The term 'item' in this system refers to loaned items owned by an organization, such as mice, monitors, PCs, books, etc.
Users belonging to the organization can [request](../../../app/Core/Request/doc.md) and borrow items as needed.

## Purpose

The purpose of the item is to manage loaned items owned by the organization.

## Functionality

The main functionalities of the item include the following:

- Item registration
- Item editing
- Item deletion

## Dependencies

Items have dependencies on [stock](../../../app/Core/Item/doc.md) quantity and [request](../../../app/Core/Request/doc.md).
